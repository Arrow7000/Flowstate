FAQs

What is Stream?

Stream is a writing app that is not for the faint of heart: if you stop writing for more than 5 seconds it will delete everything you have written. Irretrievably and irrecoverably.

We highly recommend that you start with 1 or 5 minute settings, to get used to how it works first. It is not recommended to start using this for longer than 5 minutes until you are familiar with the concept and are prepared to take on the risk of total deletion of all your work.

I've just had three hours' worth of work deleted! Can I get it back?

Unfortunately not. The 5 second timeout is a double edged sword. In order to focus the writer's mind completely, the risk has to be all-encompassing. This means that there is no way of recovering what has been deleted.

Sounds terrifying but I'll give it a go!

Great! We recommend you start with 1 minute - or 5 minutes max - session. This allows you to get a feel for the typing rhythym you should be aiming for, without risking too much.
